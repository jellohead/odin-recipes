Bowl of Cereal

Nothing fancy here. Just a plain bowl of cereal.

Ingredients:

  • Any kind of cold cereal
  • Milk

Recipe:

  1. Ready supplies
  2. Put cereal in a bowl. You decide the amount. Nobody knows how much cereal you like but you.
  3. Add enough milk to wet your cereal.
  4. Eat promptly to avoid the sog.
